About

Our Project

We are a team of passionate final-year BTech students dedicated to revolutionizing liver cancer detection. Our project combines advanced image enhancement techniques, tumor segmentation, and classification using state-of-the-art AI models. By processing CT scan images, our system identifies tumors and classifies them into three categories: Malignant, Benign, or Normal.

Features and Methods

Advanced techniques ensure precise diagnostics through enhanced imaging, detailed segmentation, and accurate categorization for effective treatment planning

Image Enhancement

Advanced enhancement of CT scan images using Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for improved clarity and diagnostic accuracy.

Tumour Segmentation

Our system isolates tumor regions from enhanced images with precision using state-of-the-art segmentation models.

Tumour Classification

Our deep learning-powered classification model determines if the tumor is Malignant, Benign, or Normal, ensuring accurate diagnosis.
